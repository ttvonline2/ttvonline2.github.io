# Questions to Ask the Interviewer Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Architecture process
- Integration pain points
- Quality validation
- Role expectations

## Questions

### Q62. How are architecture decisions made and documented in this team?
This reveals how disciplined the team is about technical direction.

### Q63. What are the biggest integration pain points in your current platform?
This helps you understand where you can add value fastest.

### Q64. How do you validate reliability and quality for system-level changes?
This shows the maturity of the team's testing and release process.

### Q65. What is the expected split between coding, architecture, and cross-team coordination for this role?
This helps you judge whether the role matches your level and goals.
