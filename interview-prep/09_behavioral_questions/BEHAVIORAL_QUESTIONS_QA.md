# Behavioral Questions Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Design from scratch
- Productivity improvements
- Debugging stories
- Influencing direction
- Changing requirements

## Questions

### Q53. Tell me about a time you designed something from scratch.
Use your FoD service or another architecture-led example and explain problem, approach, tradeoffs, and result.

### Q54. Tell me about a time you improved team productivity.
Use your developer environment setup or tooling work and explain the impact on the team.

### Q55. Tell me about a difficult debugging issue.
Pick an integration or IPC problem and explain how you narrowed root cause and validated the fix.

### Q56. Tell me about a time you influenced technical direction without formal authority.
Explain how your technical argument shaped the final design.

### Q57. Tell me about a time you handled changing requirements.
Show how you adapted while keeping architecture and delivery under control.
