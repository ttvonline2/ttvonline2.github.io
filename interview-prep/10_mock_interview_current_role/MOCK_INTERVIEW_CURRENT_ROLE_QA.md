# Mock Interview for Current Role Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Role fit
- Service design
- Architecture quality vs delivery
- AI tooling in engineering

## Questions

### Q58. Why are you a good fit for an Android Automotive role?
You bring hands-on Android services work, architecture design, IPC knowledge, automotive integration, and technical leadership.

### Q59. How would you design a new automotive system service?
Start with use cases, owners, clients, permissions, state, startup behavior, observability, and a test plan.

### Q60. How do you balance feature delivery and architecture quality?
Keep the important boundaries clean while avoiding unnecessary abstraction in low-risk areas.

### Q61. What value do AI tools or GitHub Copilot add to your workflow?
They speed up exploration, boilerplate, documentation, and repetitive coding, but still require engineering judgment.
