# System Architecture Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Linux and embedded architecture
- QNX
- Yocto
- LXC
- Containerized Android
- Automotive platform design

## Questions

### Q35. What Linux knowledge is useful for your current role?
Process behavior, permissions, logs, shell tools, boot flow, filesystems, and environment setup.

### Q36. Why does architecture matter more in embedded or automotive products?
These systems have long lifecycles, strong reliability demands, and many integration points.

### Q37. What is your experience with QNX?
QNX work in automotive usually involves system integration, IPC, performance, and Qt-based application or service development.

### Q38. How is development on QNX different from Android?
QNX feels closer to embedded systems, while Android offers a richer application framework.

### Q39. Why run Android in an LXC container on top of Yocto Linux?
It improves isolation, ownership boundaries, maintainability, and upgrade flexibility.

### Q40. What are the benefits of a containerized automotive architecture?
Better isolation, cleaner updates, clearer responsibilities, and improved long-term maintainability.
