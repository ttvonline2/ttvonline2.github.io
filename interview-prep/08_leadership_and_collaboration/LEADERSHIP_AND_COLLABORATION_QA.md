# Leadership and Collaboration Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Leadership style
- Technical ownership
- Architecture disagreements
- Cross-team coordination

## Questions

### Q49. How would you describe your leadership style?
Focus on technical clarity, removing blockers, and helping the team deliver stable outcomes.

### Q50. What is the difference between a senior engineer and a technical leader?
A technical leader adds direction, alignment, and decision-making beyond individual delivery.

### Q51. How do you handle disagreements about architecture?
Bring the discussion back to requirements, constraints, failure modes, and long-term cost.

### Q52. How do you manage work across many teams or modules?
Clarify ownership, interfaces, decisions, dependencies, and escalation paths.
