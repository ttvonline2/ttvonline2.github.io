# Performance, Reliability, and Debugging Q&A

## Covered Areas
- Performance analysis
- Optimization
- Reliability

## Questions

### Q46. How do you approach performance issues in Android or embedded systems?
Measure first, then inspect algorithmic cost, I/O, memory, IPC overhead, and threading behavior.

### Q47. Can you give an example of a practical optimization?
Use a real case such as caching, startup improvement, or reduced IPC overhead and explain the measurable result.

### Q48. How do you make systems more reliable?
Use clear state handling, input validation, recovery paths, robust logging, and simple interfaces.
